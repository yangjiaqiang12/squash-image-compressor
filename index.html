Why use Squash?

🔒

100% Private

Everything happens locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server — we can't see them even if we wanted to.

⚡

Blazing Fast

No network round-trips. Compression starts instantly using your device's hardware. Batch process dozens of images in seconds.

🎨

Multiple Formats

Convert between JPEG, PNG, and WebP. Automatically pick the best format for your use case. WebP saves 25-35% more than JPEG.

📱

Works Everywhere

Desktop, tablet, or mobile — Squash works on any modern browser. Install as a PWA for quick access anytime.

🎯

Precise Control

Adjust quality from 1-100%, set maximum dimensions, and pick your output format. You're in full control of the result.

💰

Completely Free

No usage limits, no watermarks, no hidden fees. If Squash saves you time, consider supporting the project.
